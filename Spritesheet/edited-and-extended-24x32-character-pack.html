An edited and extended version of Svetlana Kushnariova's 24x32 character pack.

Contains:

  • The 10 heroes of the original character pack (4 fighters, male & female healer, male & female mage, male & female ranger) edited to fix the problem with the walk cycles and make the walking sprites and the portraits match better.
  • The 7 nonhumans of the original character pack (flower, grass spirit, mermaid, mushroom, poprigunchik, seed, shrump), unedited.
  • The 36 NPC's of the original character pack, including 3 female aristocrats, a bard, a clown, a cultist, a dancer, a man in a mask, a flapper, a female pirate, a prince and princess, a king, a person in winter clothes, four children (two girls and two boys), two old men and an old woman, with varying degrees of editing.
  • A queen, two male nobles, and two higher ranking cultists.
  • 'Ambiguously brown' recolors of all of the heroes and most of the NPC's.
